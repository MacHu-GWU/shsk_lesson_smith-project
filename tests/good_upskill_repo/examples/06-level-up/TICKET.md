# Sum Up and Choose Your Next Step

## 1. Objective

Gather what this course taught into one clear line, confirm you are ready, and pick a direction for leveling up next.

---

## 2. What to do

1. Read this mini task's README and use it to review the whole collaboration loop.
2. Without the tutorials, explain in your own words what repository, commit, branch, Pull Request, and merge each solve.
3. From the level-up directions in the README, pick the one that interests you most and note its search keywords and one-line topic.

**Estimated time:** 15 to 30 minutes

---

## 3. Checklist

- [ ] **Can retell the loop**: without the tutorials, you can talk from creating a repo through merging, saying what each step does.
- [ ] **Concepts solid**: you can explain in your own words what each of the five core concepts solves.
- [ ] **Next step chosen**: you have picked one level-up direction and saved its keywords and topic.
